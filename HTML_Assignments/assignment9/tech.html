MSI Begins Local Manufacturing of RTX 50-Series Laptops in Chennai

MSI has announced the local production of its flagship Katana and Crosshair laptop series—powered by NVIDIA's latest GeForce RTX 50 graphics—in Chennai through a partnership with Syrma SGS Technology. This builds on MSI's previous local assembly of Modern and Katana laptops, marking a strong vote of confidence in India's manufacturing ecosystem. The initiative aims to better serve the booming demand from Indian gamers, content creators, and professionals for high-performance laptops, while potentially lowering costs and improving supply chain resilience. This move also aligns with India's growing emphasis on domestic electronics production and self-reliance in the tech sector.

IIT Madras Professor Mitesh Khapra Makes TIME's 'Top AI 100' List

Associate Professor Mitesh Khapra from IIT Madras has been named among TIME magazine's prestigious 2025 Top AI 100—a list featuring global AI luminaries. Recognized alongside leaders like Elon Musk, Sam Altman, and Mark Zuckerberg, Khapra was honoured for his groundbreaking work in democratizing AI for non-English speakers in India—making advanced technologies accessible across diverse linguistic communities. His inclusion underscores India's growing significance in inclusive AI research and highlights the increasing value placed on localization and equity in technology development.

Government to Build India's First AI-Powered Township Near Bengaluru

The Indian government has unveiled plans for the Greater Bengaluru Innovation and Tech City (GBIT)—positioned to become the nation's first AI-driven township. Located in Bidadi, about 30 km from Bengaluru, the development aims to implement a zero-carbon, zero-waste model, integrating smart urban planning with cutting-edge technologies. The project is designed to ease Bengaluru's urban congestion while setting a precedent for sustainable, scalable smart cities across India. If realized, GBIT would combine environmental responsibility with technological innovation, shaping the future of urban living.
