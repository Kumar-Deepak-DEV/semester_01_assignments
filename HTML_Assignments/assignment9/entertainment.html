'Baaghi 4' Leaks Online Hours After Release—Industry Alarmed

Tiger Shroff's highly anticipated action flick Baaghi 4 hit theaters on September 5, 2025, amid considerable fanfare—but excitement turned sour when the film was illegally leaked online within hours of release. The leak across torrent sites and streaming platforms could severely dampen box-office returns for the film, which earned around ₹11 crore on opening day despite stiff competition from The Bengal Files and The Conjuring: Last Rites. Directed by A. Harsha and featuring a high-octane cast including Sanjay Dutt, Sonam Bajwa, and Harnaaz Sandhu, the franchise's latest installment faces a major setback. The incident has reignited concerns over digital piracy's growing threat to the film industry, as producers and distributors grapple with protecting intellectual property and revenue streams.

'Saiyaara' Celebrates 50-Day Run—A Romantic Hit That Endures

Mohit Suri's romantic musical Saiyaara, featuring debut actors Ahaan Panday and Aneet Padda, has crossed a significant milestone—50 successful days in theaters as of September 5, 2025. To mark the occasion, the lead duo shared a heartfelt photograph on Instagram, embracing in front of a church with the caption, “If you believe in magic…” The film, produced by Yash Raj Films, has won over audiences with its soul-stirring music, emotional narrative, and fresh performances. Its sustained theatrical run underscores the enduring appeal of heartfelt storytelling complemented by strong musical elements, highlighting how audience connection continues to fuel box-office longevity beyond initial hype.

OTT Slate Gets Exciting with New Releases Across Genres

The lineup of upcoming OTT releases this September offers something for every viewer. Kammattam, a Malayalam crime thriller inspired by true events, premieres on ZEE5 on September 5, exploring a hit-and-run—turned-financial scam—story filmed across 40 locations in 11 days. On September 6, Rise & Fall, a reality competition on Amazon MX Player, pits 16 celebrities against each other in class-divided mental and physical challenges. Task, a psychological thriller starring Adam Driver and directed by David Fincher, debuts on September 8 via JioHotstar. Additionally, September 9 sees the return of Only Murders in the Building Season 5, featuring Steve Martin, Martin Short, and Selena Gomez in a fresh mystery-filled season. And rounding off the week, September 7 welcomes Bigg Boss Telugu Season 9 on Star Maa, promising high drama and a double-house format.
