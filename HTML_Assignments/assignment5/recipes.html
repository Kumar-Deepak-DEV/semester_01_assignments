Recipe 1: Masala Dosa

Masala dosa is a famous South Indian dish made with rice batter and spicy potato filling.

Ingredients:

  • Rice Batter
  • Potatoes
  • Spices
    • Mustard Seeds
    • Curry Leaves
    • Green Chillies
  • Oil

Cooking Steps:

  1. Prepare the rice batter
  2. Cook the potato fillings
  3. Heat the pan and spread the batter
  4. Add the filling and roll the batter
  5. After rolling, serve it hot.

Recipe 2: Paneer Butter Masala

Paneer Butter Masala is a creamy curry made with paneer, butter, and tomato gravy.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer cubes
  • Tomatoes
  • Butter
  • Spices:
    • Garam Masala
    • Chilli powder
    • Coriande powder
    • turmeric powder
    • Cloves
    • Black pepper
  • Cream

Cooking Steps:

  1. Fry the Paneer Lightly.
  2. Prepare th etomato gravy.
  3. Add butter and spices.
  4. Mix in Paneer cubes.
  5. Garnish paste with cream and coriander.

Cooking Terms:

Simmer
Cooking slowly at low temperature.
Puree
Smooth paste made from blending ingredients.


Gulab Jamun

Gulab Jamun is a sweet dessert made with milk solids, fried, and soaked in sugar syrup.

Ingredients

  • khoya(Milk Solids)
  • Maida
  • Sugar Syrup
    1. Sugar
    2. Water
    3. Cardamom
    4. Rose Water

Cooking Steps:

  1. Prepare the dough.
  2. Roll into small balls.
  3. Deep Fry buntil golden brown.
  4. Dip into sugar syrup.
  5. Serve warm.

Cooking Terms:

Knead
Working the dough to develop structure.
Syrup
A thick sweet liquid made from sugar and water.
