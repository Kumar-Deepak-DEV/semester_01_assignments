Delhi, the capital of India

Delhi, also known as 'City of cities' or 'Mini India'
It has all the different types of weather, culture, and diversity that exists in India,
and is famous for its history and historical buildings, like Red Fort, India Gate,
Qutub Minar, Jantar Mantar and so on. It also attracts people with its market like
Chandni chowk for its street food market, Sadar Bazaar for its wholesale/cheap items,
Sarojni market for the Market of clothes and the list continues.
